GDP (Gross Domestic Product)

GDP = C + I + G + (X - M). High GDP growth \uc0\u8594 More lending, more investment activity. Low GDP growth \u8594 Credit tightening, reduced financial activity. The financial system acts as an accelerator of GDP by channeling savings into productive investments.

Inflation

Sustained increase in general price level. High inflation \uc0\u8594 Central bank raises interest rates \u8594 Loans become expensive \u8594 Less borrowing. Low inflation \u8594 Lower rates \u8594 Cheap credit \u8594 More borrowing and investment. Hyperinflation destroys confidence in currency entirely.

FinTech Use: HTTPS/TLS for secure web, digital signatures, Bitcoin transactions
